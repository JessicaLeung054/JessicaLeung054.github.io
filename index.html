Jessica Leung is an undergraduate student at the University of California, Riverside. She is currently in her third year earning a BA in History and maintains a 4.0 GPA.

Jessica's area of focus is late-19th century European and American history, but she has researched a variety of different topics and time periods. She presented her research at the 20th Annual Undergraduate Research & Creative Activities Symposium, "When and Why Did Video Games Become Mainstream? Cinematic Games and Game-Like Films."
